OOPS CONCEPT
--------------------------

	Object Oriented Programing Structure
oops is a method of implementation in which programs are organised as collection of objects, class and methods

four principles oops are
	1.Abstraction
	2.Encapsulation
	3.Inheritance
	4.polymorphism
